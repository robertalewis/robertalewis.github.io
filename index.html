About

For most of my career I have been in the business of helping people get better at what they do. As a teacher, a mentor, and now a coordinator in higher education, my work has always come down to the same thing: understanding what someone needs, and building the support that gets them there.

I hold an MBA in Human Resources and a Master of Education in Teaching and Learning, so I approach talent the way I approach a classroom. Meet people where they are, design for how adults actually learn, and measure whether it worked. I am now focused on bringing that people-first perspective into human resources and talent development, where the goal is the same and the stakes are the whole organization.

Let’s build something people want to be part of.
